The '''2008–09 Minnesota Timberwolves season''' was the 20th season of the franchise in the [[National Basketball Association]] (NBA). The team  finished the season coached by [[Kevin McHale]].

==Key dates==
*'''June 26''': The [[2008 NBA Draft]] will take place in [[New York City, New York|New York City]], [[New York]].
*'''July 1''': The [[Free Agent|free agency]] period will start.
